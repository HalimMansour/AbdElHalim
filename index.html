Intro

Welcome to my portfolio! I am a skilled and enthusiastic embedded software engineer with a strong foundation in programming languages such as C, C++, and Java. My expertise extends to object-oriented programming concepts and scripting with Python, allowing me to develop robust and efficient software solutions.

Throughout my career, I have successfully worked with embedded graphics systems like LTDC, LCD-TFT, and LVGL, enabling me to create visually appealing user interfaces that enhance the user experience. I am well-versed in various communication protocols such as SPI, I2C, UART, CAN, LIN, and USB, facilitating seamless data exchange between devices.

With in-depth knowledge of real-time operating systems, including FreeRTOS, I possess the skills to develop and optimize embedded systems that prioritize reliability and responsiveness. My proficiency in embedded system design using UML state machines further enhances my ability to deliver well-structured and maintainable code.

I have a strong background in hardware-software integration, having worked on projects involving custom bootloader development and DMA programming. My experience in microcontroller interfacing, including PIC16F87XA, STM32, TIVA-C, and ESP32, has equipped me with the skills necessary to efficiently control and interface with peripheral devices.

Alongside my technical abilities, I am experienced in agile project management methodologies such as Scrum, enabling me to effectively collaborate with cross-functional teams and deliver projects within tight deadlines.

During my education, I pursued a Bachelor of Engineering in Computer Engineering at Pharos Alexandria University, achieving an Excellent with Honor. I excelled in my graduation project, which received an Excellent grade, demonstrating my dedication and proficiency in applying theoretical knowledge to practical applications.

Additionally, I have pursued further learning through relevant online courses and have earned certificates in various domains, including embedded systems software and hardware architecture, agile project management, and real-time embedded systems concepts and practices.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio, where you can explore my projects, code samples, and additional details about my skills and experiences. I am excited to connect with fellow professionals, discuss potential collaborations, and explore new opportunities.
